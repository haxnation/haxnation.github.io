Collaborative Events

Major events and partnerships that shaped our community

Mumbai FOSS 2025

April 2025

Partnered with FOSS United and Breachforce to host a cybersecurity stall at Mumbai FOSS 2025, featuring a live CTF and storyline-based challenges, engaging 50+ students and professionals.

50+ Students CTF Design Partnership

GDSC TechFest Mumbai 2024

April 2024

Co-hosted the Cybersecurity room with The Hacker's Meetup Mumbai Chapter, impacting 100+ students with sessions on SOC, Offensive Security, Blockchain Security, and Critical Infrastructure.

100+ Students 4 Sessions Partnership

Aurum Fest Cybersecurity

March 2024

Provided guidance for CTF design and development, with speaker support for Understanding SOC and CTF Introductory sessions, impacting 50+ students.

50+ Students CTF Design Speakers

GDG DevFest Mumbai

December 2023

Developed CTF challenges for DevFest Mumbai participants under The Hackers Meetup Mumbai Chapter banner, with all challenges later uploaded to our open-source repository.
