Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how refunds, cancellations, subscription terminations, and payment disputes are handled for products and services offered by Haxnation.

Important Notice: Haxnation primarily provides digital products, online subscriptions, SaaS platform access, educational content, and cybersecurity services. No physical goods are shipped unless explicitly stated.

Due to the digital and instantly accessible nature of our services, many purchases become non-refundable once access is granted or service delivery begins.

1. Products and Services Covered

This policy applies to:

  • Haxnation Jobs subscriptions
  • Job listing purchases
  • Featured listing upgrades
  • Application cap extensions
  • CTF platform subscriptions
  • Cybersecurity educational content
  • Enterprise cybersecurity services
  • Custom CTF development
  • Curriculum design and training services
  • Speaker engagements and workshops

2. Cancellation Policy

Please review this policy carefully before purchasing any service or subscription.

2.1 Digital Products & SaaS Services

Due to instant access and digital delivery, purchases for digital products and SaaS services are generally non-cancellable once payment is successfully processed.

This includes:

  • Platform subscriptions
  • Featured job listings
  • Application cap extensions
  • Digital educational content
  • Online platform access

Once access, visibility, promotion, or functionality has been activated, the transaction is considered fulfilled.

2.2 Subscription Cancellations

Recurring subscriptions may be cancelled at any time through account settings or by contacting support.

  • Cancellation prevents future billing only
  • Current billing periods remain active until expiry
  • No prorated refunds are provided for unused time
  • Cancellation must occur at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid rebilling

2.3 Enterprise & Custom Services

Cancellation terms for enterprise or custom cybersecurity services are governed by individual agreements, invoices, Statements of Work (SOW), or contracts.

Depending on project stage and resources allocated:

  • Deposits may be non-refundable
  • Completed milestones remain billable
  • Cancellation fees may apply
  • Work already delivered remains chargeable

3. Refund Policy

3.1 Non-Refundable Purchases

Unless otherwise required by applicable law, the following purchases are generally non-refundable:

  • Activated subscriptions
  • Used or active platform access
  • Completed digital deliveries
  • Featured listing purchases
  • Application cap extensions
  • Educational materials already accessed
  • Partially or fully delivered enterprise services
  • Buyer’s remorse or change of mind
  • Dissatisfaction with outcomes or hiring results

3.2 Eligible Refund Scenarios

Refunds may be considered in limited situations such as:

  • Duplicate or accidental charges
  • Unauthorized transactions (subject to verification)
  • Permanent inability to deliver paid services
  • Major technical failures caused solely by Haxnation
  • Incorrect billing caused by platform errors

For enterprise services, refunds may additionally be evaluated if:

  • Haxnation materially fails to deliver contracted work
  • Both parties mutually terminate the engagement
  • Services cannot reasonably be completed

3.3 Refund Request Timeframes

  • Digital product refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase
  • Subscription billing disputes must be raised within 7 days of the charge
  • Enterprise service disputes must follow timelines defined in the applicable agreement

Requests submitted outside applicable timeframes may not be eligible for review.

3.4 Refund Request Process

To request a refund, contact us with:

  1. Your invoice, order, or transaction ID
  2. Date of purchase
  3. Description of the issue
  4. Relevant supporting evidence or screenshots

Refund requests are reviewed individually.

We may request additional verification before approving any refund.

3.5 Refund Processing Timeline

If a refund is approved:

  • Processing may take 5–10 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method only
  • Payment processor delays are outside our control
  • Transaction or gateway fees may be non-refundable where permitted

3.6 No Return or Replacement Process

Because our products are digital and non-physical, traditional return or replacement processes do not apply.

However, if access problems occur due to platform-side technical issues, we will make reasonable efforts to restore service access or functionality.

4. Subscription Billing

4.1 Automatic Renewal

Subscription services may automatically renew unless cancelled before the next billing cycle.

By subscribing, you authorize recurring billing using your selected payment method.

4.2 Failed Payments

If recurring payments fail:

  • Access may be suspended
  • Features may become unavailable
  • Subscriptions may terminate automatically

5. Chargebacks & Payment Disputes

We encourage users to contact us directly before initiating payment disputes or chargebacks.

Fraudulent or abusive chargebacks may result in:

  • Account suspension
  • Permanent bans
  • Restriction from future purchases
  • Recovery or legal proceedings where applicable

6. Promotional Pricing & Errors

Haxnation reserves the right to correct:

  • Pricing errors
  • Technical glitches
  • Incorrect promotional displays

If an incorrect charge occurs due to a platform error, users may be offered:

  • A corrected invoice
  • Service adjustment
  • A full or partial refund where appropriate

7. Consumer Rights

Nothing in this policy limits statutory consumer rights available under applicable laws.

Users may have additional legal protections depending on their jurisdiction.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Refund & Cancellation Policy periodically.

Changes become effective once published on our website.

Continued use of Haxnation services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
